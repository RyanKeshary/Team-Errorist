Emergency Response Protocols

  • Always wear your safety gear before entering a disaster zone.
  • Never move alone — coordinate with your assigned team leader.
  • Report any hazards or missing supplies immediately through the app.
  • Stay aware of surroundings; aftershocks or secondary hazards may occur.
  • Check for pulse and breathing — administer CPR if trained.
  • Use emergency bandages to stop bleeding immediately.
  • Prioritize evacuation for victims with severe trauma.
  • Use the app’s SOS feature to request medical reinforcement.
  • Use in-app chat for team communication — avoid personal channels.
  • Report every mission update within 15 minutes of action.
  • If offline, record updates manually and sync when online.
  • Follow pre-mapped safe routes shown in the mission dashboard.
  • Assist elderly and disabled individuals first.
  • Keep evacuees calm; avoid panic through clear communication.
  • Verify headcount before marking an area as cleared.
